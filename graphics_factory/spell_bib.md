# Spell bibliography

Reference spells for CSS (and other graphics) generation. Add more entries as needed.

---

## Fire

- **Fireball** — Classic exploding sphere of flame.
- **Inferno** — Sustained wall of fire.
- **Ember rain** — Falling sparks and small flames.

## Ice

- **Frost bolt** — Single shot of freezing energy.
- **Blizzard** — Swirling snow and ice.
- **Permafrost** — Ground crack and ice spread.

## Lightning

- **Chain lightning** — Bolt that jumps between targets.
- **Thunder strike** — Single loud strike from above.
- **Static field** — Crackling electric aura.

## Earth / Nature

- **Earthquake** — Ground shake and fissures.
- **Vine whip** — Plants lashing out.
- **Stone skin** — Defensive rock shell.

## Chaos / Meta

- **Chaos orb** — Unpredictable multicolor burst.
- **Emotional damage** — Invisible psychic hit.
- **Rain of cats** — Cats falling from the sky.
