# Emoji effects list

Canonical list of effects for the emoji tool. Each effect defines how particles move on screen.

---

- **rain** — Fall from top, gentle rotation and fade (classic spell rain).
- **burst** — Scale from center with fade (instant pop).
- **spiral** — Rotate outward from center (whirlwind).
- **pulse** — Scale in place, breathing effect.
- **wave** — Horizontal wave motion (left–right sway).
- **explode** — Outward from center with fast scatter.
